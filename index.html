Meet Our Founders

I started Indalnova at just 16 because i was tired of shortcuts and false promises in skincare. I wanted to solve real skin problems with real ingredients—pure, honest, and effective.

Our mission is simple: to make skincare in India trustworthy, effective, and affordable for everyone. At Indalnova, we believe in harmonizing nature with science, so your skin feels confident every single day.

For us, it’s not just about products—it’s about creating a lifestyle of self-love and purity. That’s why we stay true to our core values: sustainability, quality, safety, and community. We responsibly source our ingredients, keep our formulations clean and toxin-free, and give back through wellness and empowerment initiatives.

Every drop of Indalnova is crafted with care—lightweight, plant-based, cruelty-free, and made for all skin types, especially sensitive skin. Our products are, created in small batches, and designed for glow, balance,and long-term skin health.

Indalnova is not just skincare—it’s our promise to nurture skin, naturally.

— Aniket Singh Thakur, Founders of Indalnova
